Benchmarking of taxonomic profilers Visualizer

Benchpro VIZard is the visualization tool for benchpro, a taxonomic profiler benchmarking tool. Please follow the manual to generate the correct data for your own target tool, or load the example data containing many of the most used taxonomic profilers such as mOTUs3, MetaPhlAn 4, bracken, sylph, and more.
